System Stability Program

Join the System Stability Program to us help troubleshoot our Nothing OS issues faster.

System error log information will be shared with Nothing Technology Limited (“NOTHING”) and its affiliates and agents with your permission. During the process of submitting error logs for your device, you may in certain rare cases be required to provide personal information such as telephone number, email address, and Facebook account details. NOTHING only keeps this data for the analysis period. This data will not be associated with any personal identity recognition and will not be shared with any third-party partners for any purpose. Declining to join the System Stability Program will not affect your other services from NOTHING. After choosing to share error data with NOTHING, you can stop sharing through the following method: Setting-Privacy--Experience improvement programs -System Stability Program and turn off the “System Stability Program” service. Please refer to NOTHING Privacy Policy to learn more about more about how we handle your personal information and protect its security. Once you withdraw from the program, NOTHING will stop collecting the information mentioned above.
