User Experience Program

Join the User Experience Program to help the Nothing OS   get better.

NOTHING will collect your phone usage information to improve product design and user experience with your permission, If you agree to join the User Experience Program(“Program”), this indicates that you agree that NOTHING may collect, use and analyze the following information:

  1. User behavior data.
  2. Configuration data.
  3. Statistics data of application program.

By collecting and analyzing the above data, NOTHING aims to improve the user experience, such as improved system stability and battery efficiency. This data does not identify you personally, nor will NOTHING link it to other data that identifies you personally. It is linked to your device’s unique identifier, which you can change either by resetting your device such as factory reset, clean user data…etc. We use reasonable safeguards, such as encryption, to protect the data. After you join the Program, you can withdraw at any time through the following method: Settings-Privacy-Data Collection and turn off the “ User Experience Program” service. Please refer to the NOTHING Privacy Policy to learn more about the information processed by NOTHING. Once you withdraw from the Program, NOTHING will stop collecting your information mentioned above.
